# Medusa Drink House - Design System

## 1. Vibe & Atmosphere
- **Mood**: Luxury, Mysterious, Dark, High-Energy.
- **Theme**: Dark Mode (Background: #0a0a0a).
- **Core Elements**: Snakes, Neon, Stone Glow.

## 2. Palette
- **Primary**: #06ef6b (Medusa Neon Green) - Used for primary actions, glow effects, and highlights.
- **Background**: #0a0a0a (Deep Black).
- **Surface**: #141414 (Dark Charcoal) - Used for cards and containers.
- **Text**: #ffffff (Primary), #999999 (Secondary).

## 3. Typography
- **Headlines**: 'Outfit' - Heavy weights (700-900) for a modern, bold look.
- **Body**: 'Inter' - Regular weights for high readability.

## 4. Components Style
- **Cards**: Glassmorphism with thin borders (rgba(255,255,255,0.1)).
- **Buttons**: Rounded-12px, high-contrast.
- **Glows**: Subtle radial gradients with #06ef6b.

## 5. Metadata for Stitch Generations
Add this to every prompt:
`Platform: Web, Mobile-first. Palette: Medusa Neon Green (#06ef6b), Deep Black (#0a0a0a). Styles: Glassmorphism, 12px roundness.`
